Release 0: Pseudocode for using an elevator

Enter elevator
Turn left
Move forward until you reach wall
Turn left
Move forward until you reach the next wall (assuming the panel is in the corner)
Reach down to the floor
Extending your index finger
Until you reach a button AND the button is the desired number
    Move finger from the left side of the panel to the right side of the panel
    Move finger up one inch
Push the button
