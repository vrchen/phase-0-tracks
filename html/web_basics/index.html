Welcome to my snowboarding world. To learn how to snowboard, it is very important that you are fit and willing to take a tumble!

This is my list of things to bring on a trip

  • snowboard
  • boots
  • jacket
  • pants
  • helmet
  • gloves
  • goggles

These are the steps for planning a trip

  1. Find friends
  2. Find a date that works for everyone
  3. Book lodging
  4. Drive/fly there
  5. Ski/snowboard!
